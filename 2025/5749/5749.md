### Security Bulletin: NVIDIA Isaac Launchable - December 2025

Updated 2025-12-23

NVIDIA has released an update for the NVIDIA Isaac Launchable to address a security issue that might lead to the impacts described in this bulletin.<br><div>To protect your system, download and install the <a href="https://github.com/isaac-sim/isaac-launchable">latest version of Isaac Launchable.</a></div>

Go to [NVIDIA Product Security](https://www.nvidia.com/security/)
