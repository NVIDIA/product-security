### Security Bulletin: NVIDIA TensorRT - May 2026

Updated 2026-05-19

NVIDIA has released a software update for NVIDIA® TensorRT.&nbsp;<div>To protect your system, clone or update this software to TensorRT v10.16.1 from the <a href="https://github.com/NVIDIA/TensorRT">NVIDIA/TensorRT</a> GitHub repo, <a href="https://developer.nvidia.com/tensorrt/download/10x">TensorRT product page</a> or the <a href="https://developer.download.nvidia.com/compute/cuda/repos/">network repository</a>.</div>

Go to [NVIDIA Product Security](https://www.nvidia.com/security/)

_______________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

### Details

This section provides a summary of potential vulnerabilities that this security update addresses and their impact. Descriptions use [CWE™](https://cwe.mitre.org/), and base scores and vectors use [CVSS v3.1](https://www.first.org/cvss/specification-document) standards.

| **CVE ID** | **Description** | **Vector** | **Base Score** | **Severity** | **CWE** | **Impacts** |
| ---------- | ---------------- | ---------- | -------------- | ------------ | -------- | ------------ |
| CVE-2026-24188 | NVIDIA TensorRT contains a vulnerability where an attacker could cause an out-of-bounds write. A successful exploit of this vulnerability might lead to data tampering. | [CVSS:3.1/AV:N/AC:L/PR:N/UI:N/S:U/C:N/I:H/A:L](https://www.first.org/cvss/calculator/3.1#CVSS:3.1/AV:N/AC:L/PR:N/UI:N/S:U/C:N/I:H/A:L) | 8.2 | HIGH | [CWE-787](https://cwe.mitre.org/data/definitions/787.html) | Data tampering, denial of service |

The NVIDIA risk assessment is based on an average of risk across a diverse set of installed systems and may not represent the true risk to your local installation. NVIDIA recommends evaluating the risk to your specific configuration.
