### Security Bulletin: NVIDIA AIStore Framework - June 2026

Updated 2026-06-30

NVIDIA has released an update for the NVIDIA AIStore™ framework to address a security issue that might lead to the impacts described in this bulletin.<div>To protect your system, download and install the <a href="https://github.com/NVIDIA/aistore/releases/tag/v1.4.5">latest version of the NVIDIA AIStore</a> framework.</div>

Go to [NVIDIA Product Security](https://www.nvidia.com/security/)

### Details

This section provides a summary of potential vulnerabilities that this security update addresses and their impact. Descriptions use [CWE&trade;](https://cwe.mitre.org/), and base scores and vectors use [CVSS v3.1](https://www.first.org/cvss/specification-document) standards.

| **CVE ID** | **Description** | **Vector** | **Base Score** | **Severity** | **CWE** | **Impacts** |
| ---------- | ---------------- | ---------- | -------------- | ------------ | -------- | ------------ |
| CVE-2026-24270 | NVIDIA AIStore framework contains a vulnerability where an attacker could bypass authentication. A successful exploit of this vulnerability might lead to denial of service, escalation of privileges, information disclosure, and data tampering. | [CVSS:3.1<br>/AV:N<br>/AC:L<br>/PR:N<br>/UI:N<br>/S:U<br>/C:H<br>/I:H<br>/A:H](https://www.first.org/cvss/calculator/3.1#CVSS:3.1/AV:N/AC:L/PR:N/UI:N/S:U/C:H/I:H/A:H) | 9.8 | CRITICAL | [CWE-290](https://cwe.mitre.org/data/definitions/290.html) | Denial of service, escalation of privileges, information disclosure, data tampering |

The NVIDIA risk assessment is based on an average of risk across a diverse set of installed systems and may not represent the true risk to your local installation. NVIDIA recommends evaluating the risk to your specific configuration.
