### Security Bulletin: NVIDIA BlueField 2 and 3 BMC - January 2024

Updated 2024-01-23

NVIDIA has released a firmware update for NVIDIA BlueField DPU Baseboard Management Controller (BMC).<div>To protect your system, download and install this firmware update from the <a href="https://developer.nvidia.com/networking/doca">NVIDIA DOCA Software Framework</a> page.</div>

Go to [NVIDIA Product Security](https://www.nvidia.com/security/)

### Details
